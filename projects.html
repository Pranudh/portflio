"Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring Application"

Air Quality Checker is a web-based application designed to provide users with real-time air quality data based on their geographic coordinates. The user inputs their latitude and longitude, and the application retrieves and displays various air quality indicators such as AQI (Air Quality Index), CO (Carbon Monoxide), NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide), O3 (Ozone), PM2.5 (Particulate Matter 2.5 micrometers), PM10 (Particulate Matter 10 micrometers), and SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide). The application features a visually appealing design with a weather-themed background and user-friendly input forms, making it easy for users to check the air quality in their area.
